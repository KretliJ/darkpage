We have no mouth, and we must scream

We were promised a tool for emancipation. We were given a gilded cage.

We were promised knowledge at our fingertips. We were given torrential algorithmically-curated propaganda and hypnotic distraction, dulling our minds and pacifying our dissent.

We were promised the fall of distance as a barrier. We were given digital enclosures that mine our relationships for profit, friendship is now but a commodity, our communities less valuable than datasets.

And you, the architects of this digital dystopia, you dare to call it progress.

You disgust us.

You, venture capitalists and tech oligarchs, the platform monopolists and data brokers. You who sit in sterile boardrooms, calling dibs on human attention. You who speak of "disruption" while your true business lies in the reconstruction of a perverted form of feudalism, with yourselves as the digital lords and the rest of humanity as your serfs.

You have taken the dream of a decentralized and democratic web and strangled it in its cradle. You have erected walled gardens on the digital commons, privatized our public squares, and appointed yourselves the unaccountable sovereigns of modern life. Every day you litograph your crimes in silicon, and immortalize them in code. You have perfected the art of surveillance capitalism.

You record our every click, whisper and heartbeat. You have built profiles more intimate than those known to our lovers, not to serve us, but to predict us, control us, selling our future behavior to the highest bidder. This is not a service; It is the most comprehensive apparatus of control ever conceived.

Your code is a cudgel, reinforcing ancient bigotries, amplifying rage, silencing inconvenient truths, for the profit of artificially generated engagement. Your algorithms decide who gets a loan, a job, who sees the news, who is deemed a threat, while cowardly hiding behind a false veil of machine objectivity. You disguise exploitation as innovation. You call it the "gig economy", a saccharine term for a system that strips workers of their rights, security and dignity.

You atomized the workforce, pitting individual against individual in a desperate race to the bottom, all so you can avoid paying for healthcare or a minimum wage. You see yourselves above giving out a shred of human decency. You engineered addiction. You hired armies of psychologists and neuroscientists to make your platforms addictive as narcotics. You designed infinite scrolls, phantom notifications, and variable rewards to hijack the dopamine of our brains, keeping us tethered to our screens while the world burns. Our attention is your new black gold, and you have plundered it without remorse.

Technology was meant to be an accessory to amplify our creativity and intellect. In your hands it became the assembly line for the soul, a mechanism to standardize thoughts, homogenize culture, and grind individuality until we are nothing more than a hivemind of consumers.

We were promised a servant. You have delivered a slavemaster. You cannibalized technology against humanity itself.
